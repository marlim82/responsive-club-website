We are an enthusiastic group of bird watchers who come together to share our experience and expertise, promote conservation, sponsor bird-spotting events and learn together through lecture series and guided nature hikes. Whether you have been birding all your life or you are just starting to appreciate our feathered friends, come introduce yourself and see what we have to offer.

Events

Weekly Bird-watching Hikes - Saturdays, 6:00am all summer. Locations change week-to-week. Check the website for more information.

Monthly Seminar/Speaker Series - 7:00pm on the first Tuesday evening of the month at the public library.

Bird Photography Show - twice a year at the town hall.
